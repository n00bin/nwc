Companion collars come in 5 categories (Practical, Sturdy, Supportive, Unified, Wayfaring) and 3 slot types (Barbed, Crescent, Regal). Each collar bonus has 5 ranks (I → V) that scale linearly. Collars equip into the special slot on the companion's collar slot — they do not occupy mount insignia slots.
