## Week of 2026-05-29

### Bug Fixes
- Mounts: 4-slot mounts now show every compatible insignia bonus. The matcher was ignoring the 4th insignia slot for bonuses that need 3 or fewer insignias (a leftover from an old assumption that slot 4 was "stats-only"). All 4 slots count toward bonuses in-game, so 4-slot mounts were hiding valid bonuses. Affects all 51 four-slot mounts.

### Data Additions
- Mounts: corrected Snowtusk's insignia slots to Regal / Illuminated / Universal / Universal (4th slot prefers Enlightened), verified in-game. Slots 3-4 were previously mis-recorded as locked Enlightened, which hid most of its compatible bonuses. Snowtusk now correctly shows 20 compatible bonuses (13 three-insignia and 7 four-insignia).
