## Week of July 5, 2026

### Features
- **Toon Forge: new builds start with zero buffs.** Campfire and VIP Rank 3 are no longer pre-checked on a fresh or cleared build — the Detailed Stats panel now shows only what your character itself provides until you tick the buffs you actually run. Saved builds and share links are unaffected (they keep the buffs they were made with).

### Data Additions
- **Runefrost Lightstaff (Cleric Main Hand) added** from a player report — IL 5500, part of the Chilling Flow set from Jotunskar (Advanced). The reported stats matched the Runefrost weapon family's stat ladder exactly. (Report #133) Now also screenshot-verified in-game — every value confirmed.
- **Cleric weapons: three more added from in-game tooltips** — Runefrost Devotion Emblem (Off Hand, IL 5500, Report #134), plus the Master-tier pair Wintermarked Rimecrook (Main Hand, IL 5800) and Wintermarked Sanctum Emblem (Off Hand, IL 5800). Cleric endgame weapons went from 2 to 6 in the database.

### Bug Fixes
- **Chilling Flow set text corrected on all 15 Runefrost weapons.** In-game tooltips revealed the per-stack bonuses differ by weapon tier: the Advanced (Runefrost) tier grants 0.35% Power / 0.5% Crit Severity / 0.4% Overall Outgoing Healing / 0.4% Awareness per stack — our pages had been showing the Master (Wintermarked) tier's higher numbers on all tiers. Frostbound-tier values are marked unverified until someone captures that tooltip.
