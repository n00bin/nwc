## Week of 2026-06-05 (since last publish)

### Features
- **Clearer everywhere — audit polish pass.** The home page's Artifacts card now shows live database counts (138 artifacts & 38 sets — the old hardcoded label said 61 & 18!). Mount ranking tabs and insignia filters explain themselves on hover. Profession table headers spell out Proficiency / Commission / Skill Chance. Links into Mekaniks now open the right tab. The one unverified campaign booster is clearly marked UNCONFIRMED. And on phones, Toon Forge now shows its "Experimental" explanation instead of hiding it behind a hover tooltip.

### Data Additions
- **Toon Forge — Soulweaver Warlocks get their missing class features.** The Soulweaver class-feature picker only offered its 4 path-specific picks (Borrowed Spirit, Flowing Link, Souleater, Soulbond) — but in game the path also slots the 4 class-shared features. Archived Powers-tab screenshots from the original capture confirmed all four on the Soulweaver tab: Flames of Empowerment, Dark One's Blessing (generates Soulweave on this path), Dust to Dust (+5% damage), and Shadow Walk (+10% Movement, +2.5% Deflect & Critical Strike). All four are now pickable on Soulweaver, and the stat-granting ones count in your build math. The Hellbringer's Soul Puppet mechanic (the summon several Warlock powers create, with its Soul Investiture stacking) was also added to the database from the same screenshot batch.
- **Three missing Jotunskar pants added.** The Cracked-tier pants from the Jotunskar (Master) clothing collection are now in the database: Cracked Lifebraid Vestment (Survivor's Gift), Cracked Stormbind Tunic (Reckless Advantage), and Cracked Aetherwrap (Challenger's Awareness), all IL 4,600. They share names with their shirt counterparts but carry different bonuses — the Pants picker now shows them.
- **Reports #87, #88, #90 — three Barbarian/Fighter gear reports resolved.** The Harness of the Flayed Legion was missing its +1,968 Combat Advantage stat and wasn't marked Barbarian-only — both fixed (Report #87). The DPS version of the Prismatic Crystalgard Gauntlets (Enveloped Rage — stacking Critical Severity when fighting 3+ enemies) is now in the database for Barbarians and Fighters; we previously only had the Tank and Healer versions (Report #88). And the Knot of the Bloodbound off-hand is confirmed complete at all 6 upgrade tiers, closing out the last of the Barbarian weapon reports (Report #90).
- **Bloodwoven Brands (Warden's Defense version) added.** The Doomvault Remains clothing piece with the Warden's Defense bonus (gain 5% Defense for 10s when a single hit takes more than 15% of your Max HP) is now in the gear database as a Pants item — the database previously only had the Depleted Defiance version. Also from the same screenshot batch: the Enchanted Forte 2-piece set bonus was corrected to +3,000 Forte (it was listed as +1,000), the Enchanted Advantage bonus is confirmed as +3,000 Accuracy, and the Dark Magic Defence set's bonus is +2% Awareness.

### Features (cont.)
- **Toon Forge — weapon Artifact Modifications, the full picture.** The Off Hand carries BOTH stat modifications. "Art Mod 1" is a simple pick — each stat grants a fixed, owner-verified value shown right in the menu: Action Point Gain +2.5%, Recharge Speed +2.5%, Stamina Regeneration +5%, Control Bonus +1,200, Control Resist +1,200, Incoming Healing +1,400, Forte +1,100, or Critical Severity +1,400 — no number to type. "Art Mod 2" stays pick-a-stat + enter your upgraded value (clamped to its real max). The Main Hand gets the real thing instead: the in-game "Artifact Modification Management" pick — one active **Enhanced power** (+10% damage or healing to one specific power). All six Warlock options are in from owner screenshots (Enhanced Dark Spiral, Hellish Rebuke, Hand of Blight, Infernal Barrier, Eldritch Blast, Soul Reconstruction); pick a damage one and the damage sim boosts that power by 10% while you have it slotted. Healing and per-stack picks (like Dark Spiral's bonus inside Dark Helix) are recorded on your build but honestly excluded from the sim. Other classes' Enhanced menus need screenshots before they appear. Saves & share links carry all three weapon mods.
- **Toon Forge — Artifact Modification support (Off Hand).** Artifact off-hands carry a player-set modification — a stat of your choice with a value you've upgraded in game. The Off Hand slot now has an "Art Mod 2" control: pick the stat (Accuracy, Awareness, Critical Strike, Critical Avoidance, Deflect, Power, Health, Combat Advantage, Deflect Severity, or Defense) and enter your current value — it's clamped to each stat's real in-game max (verified values, e.g. Deflect 6,800 / Health 24,200) and feeds straight into your stat sheet and Max HP. Saves and share links carry it. Main-hand modification support comes next.
- **Toon Forge — multi-tier gear is now ONE picker row with a dropdown.** Items that exist at several upgrade tiers (Doomcleaver's six qualities, tiered legacy weapons, refinable armor) used to fill the picker with near-identical rows — 316 item families were taking up 1,101 rows. Each family is now a single row with an "Upgrade tier" dropdown; pick the tier you own and the card's stats, Combined Rating, and set info update live; clicking equips exactly that tier (and your share links carry it). Same-name items that are different VERSIONS rather than tiers (like a piece's DPS / Tank / Healer variants at the same item level) get a "Version" dropdown labeled by their equip bonus instead, so they're never confused with upgrades.
- **Toon Forge — weapon tiers now show their refinement quality.** Set weapons refine from green Uncommon upward (✦ Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic → Celestial), and the picker now labels every tier accordingly — modern six-tier ladders like Doomcleaver and Eye of the Doomweaver top out at ✦ Celestial, while older shorter ladders label up from Uncommon as far as they go. The quality label also replaces the Combined Rating readout on those rows, so you can grab exactly the version you own at a glance. Same-item-level bonus variants are never mistaken for upgrade ladders.

### Bug Fixes
- **Prismatic Luminstep Greaves no longer read wrong.** This Dread Sanctum (Master) feet piece comes in two versions, and the database had both of them garbled plus a stray third. Now fixed: the DPS version (+3,330 Critical Strike / +2,498 Forte, Discharged Precision bonus — verified against tooltip screenshots) and the healer version (Ruthless Resources — owner-confirmed in game), whose bonus text used to cut off mid-sentence ("…your Divinity/Performance/Soulweave regeneration for 15 seconds") and now reads the full effect: damage or heal for more than 10% of your Max HP in one blow and you gain 5% faster Divinity/Performance/Soulweave regeneration for 15s, stacking up to 5. A true duplicate of the DPS version listing 3,530 Critical Strike (200 too high) was removed. The two real versions now show as separate, clearly-labeled picker entries.
- **Impending Doom weapon set — real in-game text and values on all 84 Soul Collector weapons.** The 2-piece set bonus had been paraphrased with wrong numbers (two entries even disagreed: "13 Charges" vs "18 Charges"). An owner screenshot of the equipped tooltip set it straight: accumulate **10 Charges** to become Unleashed (1/Daily on 10s CD, 1/Encounter on 1s CD, 1 per 5s in combat; lasts 20s, drops when you leave combat; DPS get +4.5% Base Damage Boost, Healers +4.5% Outgoing Healing) — plus always-on **+7.5% Critical Severity** (we had 2.5!) and +2.5% Power. Every class's weapon pair now carries the exact wording, the stats count in Toon Forge (role-aware), and two classes' pairs that were silently double-counting the set are fixed. Also from the same screenshots: Omen of Doom's missing Critical Severity and +100 Damage lines, and the Whisper of Power / Impending Doom set names being swapped on two tiers.
- **Four Deep-Riven Jotunskar pants were filed under the wrong slot.** Player screenshots of the Jotunskar (Master) pants collection confirmed Deep-Riven Aetherwrap, Lifebraid Vestment, Stormbind Tunic (all listed as Shirt) and Titanweave Harness (listed as Armor) are actually Pants — the Pants picker now shows all four. Their tooltips also verified a small stat fix (Lifebraid Vestment Power is 1,273, not 1,275) and their equip bonuses (Challenger's Awareness, Survivor's Gift, Reckless Advantage, Ruthless Critical) now count in Toon Forge stat math instead of being description-only.
- **A lost Jotunskar pants piece is back in the picker.** The Veinlit Stormbind Tunic (IL 3,800) had an invalid slot in the database, so no slot picker ever showed it. A player screenshot from the Reghed Edge collection confirmed it's Pants — and along the way fixed its Accuracy (1,852, not 1,862), added its missing Precision Tactics bonus (+10,625 Critical Strike while Stamina is over 75%), and recorded where it drops (The Biting Cold Heroic Encounters).
- **Jotunskar clothing set tags straightened out.** The "Winterworn Harness" tag on 16 Jotunskar clothing pieces was the collection tab name, not a real set — each piece now shows its true 2-piece set (Freezing Energy / Grasp / Advantage / Touch / Stand / Fortitude / Rage). Archived screenshots also proved those sets only exist on the top-tier (IL 4,850) versions, so phantom set tags were removed from 28 lower-tier pieces (Cracked, Runemarked, Veinlit) that never had a set bonus in-game.
- **Item notes cleaned up site-wide.** Notes on gear, companions, mounts, powers, enchants, and consumables no longer show internal data-entry bookkeeping ("Screenshot intake…", "Screenshot confirmed…"). You now see only the parts that matter — tooltip details, restrictions, and game behavior. The underlying database still keeps full records behind the scenes.
- **Toon Forge — mount power rarity now counts in your Total Item Level.** Player verification showed a Celestial mount's combat and equip powers are each IL 3,937 (vs 3,000 at Mythic) — the builder was always counting them at their stored value regardless of your rarity setting. If you run Celestial active mount powers, your TIL just gained ~1,874 (and with it ~18,700 Max HP). Also fixed along the way: eleven mount powers whose values had been captured at Celestial were double-scaling when you picked Celestial (and showing Celestial numbers under the Mythic setting) — Pack Tactics among them. Each value now lands on the right number at every rarity.
- **Dragonflight Ward Necklace stat corrected.** In-game verification showed +529 Critical Avoidance (the database said Defense); fixed, and a duplicate entry was removed. Two long-suspicious companion powers were also checked in-game and confirmed CORRECT as stored: Raptor's Instincts (4.5% Power per Pack stack — it stacks ×5 party-wide) and Bobby's Vigor (+12,000 Max HP — an intentional outlier like Energon).
- **Toon Forge — Max HP was counting Constitution twice.** The engine applied CON's +0.5%-per-point HP bonus in two different places, so every Max HP figure ran about 7% high (at CON 14: ×1.07 applied twice). Found in the June 5 full-site audit; one of the two paths removed and verified with a before/after check. Max HP numbers across all builds are now slightly lower and correct.
- **Friendlier error messages site-wide.** The patch notes page now tells you when it couldn't load (instead of showing a blank page), and the Reports page speaks plain English when something fails ("Couldn't update the status — check your connection and try again") instead of raw technical codes. Replying on one report no longer locks the reply box on every other report.
- **Toon Forge — share links now carry your damage-sim settings.** The Combat Advantage uptime slider and sim magnitude were being dropped from saved builds and share links, silently resetting to defaults on load. They're included now.
- **Reports page — newest reports now sort to the top.** A flipped sort order was pinning "In Progress" reports first and burying brand-new ones at the bottom of the active list. New > Confirmed > In Progress now, as intended. Also fixed: deleting a community reply as admin no longer triggers a stray second "delete this report?" prompt.
- **Toon Forge — small stat-accuracy fixes from the audit.** 66 items whose tooltip bonuses like "+1.5% Movement Speed" had been stored as (worthless) rating points now count as the real percent bonuses; 13 of them were even counted twice. Renamed items in old saved builds now reliably load the right item tier, and the 🔍 gear details popup now shows the tier you actually picked instead of the first one in the database.
- **Gear database cleanup (June 5 audit).** Corrected 9 items whose stored item level was wrong (four Aegis of the Condemned tiers were saved with their rarity tier instead of the real IL 3750–4800; five Tempest Gaze accessories were IL 1150 instead of 3150), fixed one wrong Combined Rating, re-linked two Impending Doom weapons whose set bonus pointed at the wrong set name, and removed 33 duplicate gear entries that were cluttering the Toon Forge pickers. Stat names were also normalized to the exact in-game spellings ("Deflect", "Control Resist") across 800+ entries.
- **Reports #95–#97 — Cerebral Predator Ink moved to the Pants slot.** Two players reported this Dread Sanctum collection piece as "missing" — it was in the database, but filed under Shirt, so the Pants picker never showed it. Collection tooltips don't state the slot, and the original intake guessed wrong. The Combat Advantage/Critical Severity and Combat Advantage/Awareness versions are now Pants (player-verified); the Critical Strike/Defense version is flagged for an in-game slot check since slots can differ per variant.
